About Us

One organization dedicated to feeding street dogs and cats is "Paws & Whiskers Foundation." Founded in 2010, they operate in urban areas where stray animals face hunger and neglect. With a team of volunteers, they provide regular feeding programs, offering nutritious meals to stray dogs and cats. Additionally, they offer veterinary care, including vaccinations and spaying/neutering services, to promote the health and well-being of these animals. Through community outreach and education, they aim to raise awareness about responsible pet ownership and advocate for the humane treatment of all animals. Paws & Whiskers Foundation embodies compassion and commitment to improving the lives of street animals.
